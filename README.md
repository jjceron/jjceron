# About Me

I am a **Machine Learning Researcher** focused on the development of intelligent methods for biomedical signal analysis, statistical learning, and scientific computing.

My research combines **machine learning**, **deep learning**, **signal processing**, and **probabilistic modeling** to design robust, interpretable, and reproducible AI systems capable of extracting meaningful information from complex biomedical data.

My current interests include representation learning, uncertainty-aware AI, multimodal learning, and explainable machine learning for healthcare applications.
